Twilight Tangle: Conquering the Lights Out Challenge

The goal of the Lights Out game is to switch off all the lights on a grid. When you click on a square, it changes its state, along with the states of its neighboring squares. For instance, if you click on a lit square, it turns off, and its adjacent squares switch - from on to off or vice versa. This creates a puzzle where you need to plan your clicks strategically to turn off all lights on the grid.
